Special Offers

  • Loyalty discount — 40% off future sessions in the same zone after completing both initial session + touch-up.

Refresh Policy

  • Refresh appointments are available from 2 months after the initial procedure and at any future time.
  • Refresh investment is 40% off the original cost for the same zone (brows, lips, or eyes).
  • To qualify, both the initial session and the touch-up must have been completed at Anatomy PMU.
  • If a procedure is done elsewhere after the initial session, the refresh rate no longer applies.

Additional Notes

  • All procedures include full consultation, mapping, pigment selection, and aftercare instructions.
  • Previous PMU from another artist may require evaluation and is priced as new work.
  • Final investment may vary based on complexity — old PMU, asymmetry, or color correction.
  • SMP and removal may require multiple sessions for optimal results.
