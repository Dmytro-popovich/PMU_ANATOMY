Studio Policies

Clear, fair and client-friendly.

Read before booking

Deposits, cancellations, touch-ups, health & eligibility, conduct, and liability.

Deposits & Payments
  • Non-refundable deposit secures your appointment (applied to total).
  • Deposits: $100 per zone for procedures; $50 per zone for touch-ups.
  • Balance due on the day of procedure.
  • Payments: Zelle, Venmo, Cash App, cash or card.
Cancellations & Rescheduling
  • 72+ hours: deposit stays on your balance for a new date.
  • <72 hours or no-show: deposit is forfeited. New booking requires a new deposit.
Touch-Up & Loyalty
  • PMU is a two-step process. Touch-up at 5–7 weeks: $150.
  • After 8 weeks additional fees may apply.
  • Annual Refresh (6–15 months): 40% off current full price (same zone only).
  • Work done by another artist voids loyalty pricing; charged as new work.
Previous Work by Another Artist
  • Must be disclosed before booking.
  • Corrections/cover-ups are priced as new work; consultation may be required.
Health & Eligibility
  • No PMU if: under 18, pregnant/breastfeeding, uncontrolled diabetes/bleeding/healing disorders, active skin issues in area, keloid tendency, current illness, or under influence.
  • Accutane: stop 12 months prior. Retinol/actives: stop 7 days prior.
  • Blood thinners 48h prior (unless prescribed — procedure may be declined).
Studio Conduct & Liability
  • Pets and children are welcome. Partners may attend only if booked.
  • Silence your phone during the procedure.
  • By booking, you confirm accurate medical disclosure and agree that results depend on aftercare and individual healing.
